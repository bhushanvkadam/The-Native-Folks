Questions We Love Getting

Everything you want to know before your first bite.

What makes your raisins different from store-bought ones?
Most commercial raisins are sulphur-treated to achieve that golden colour and preserve shelf life. Ours are 100% sun-dried over 7 days with zero sulphur, zero bleaching, and no chemical treatment — which is why they're naturally dark and taste like real fruit. We also grow in Jeevamrit-enriched soil, which means richer flavour from the ground up.
Are your raisins certified organic?
Yes. We are FSSAI licensed (Lic. No. 11225032000017) and our produce is grown using certified organic farming practices — no synthetic pesticides, no chemical fertilisers. Every batch is clinically lab-tested for residue before it reaches your door.
How should I store the raisins to keep them fresh?
Our packs come with a resealable moisture-barrier zipper. Once opened, press out the air, seal tight, and store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight. They stay fresh for up to 6 months this way. For longer storage, you can refrigerate them — they'll keep for up to a year.
What is the best way to eat them?
The most popular way among our customers is soaking 10–15 raisins overnight in water and eating them first thing in the morning on an empty stomach — great for digestion and iron absorption. You can also eat them straight as a snack, add them to oats, kheer, pulao, or trail mixes. No wrong answer here.
Where are the grapes sourced from?
Our grapes come from the Nashik Valley in Maharashtra — India's wine and raisin capital, known for its rich basalt soil and ideal dry climate. We work directly with small family farms who share our commitment to chemical-free, traditional farming. No middlemen, no compromises.
How long does delivery take?
On Amazon, orders typically arrive within 1–2 days for Prime members. On Blinkit, delivery is in under 10 minutes in serviceable areas. For WhatsApp orders, we dispatch within 24 hours from Mumbai, Pune, and Bangalore — delivery usually takes 2–3 business days depending on your location.
